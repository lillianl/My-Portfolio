The Super Secret Surprise Birthday Party

Emotional Responsibility and Consent

This is a children's book for kids around the ages of 5-6. It presents the concept of good touch, bad touch, good secrets, and bad secrets using the analogy of a surprise birthday party.


Good sex ed introduction is serverely lacking in the United States. The Netherlands's sex education system begins its schooling as early as Kindergarten. It starts an important conversation behind self image and gender stereotypes. The illustrations in this book address how a child should respond when they experience bad touch, by learning how to say no and identifying people to trust.


The key challenge in this set was to make all the illustrations, relatable to children of all backgrounds. My client and I discussed race and gender stereotypes, and how color and shape can influence first impressions.
